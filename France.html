France, home to the French

France is one of the most populous countries and the third largest country in Europe. France has a variety of climates and is a very popular tourist destination. It is home to the Alps, plains, and beaches on both the Meditterean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. The heart of French Culture is in Paris, their capital and center of all French politics and culture.



France's current President as of 2024 is Emmanuel Macron and it's main currency is the Euro. It's language French, is one of the most popular languages in the entire world and is used by several other countries excluding France. One of the reasons France's influence reaches so far is their former colonial empire. The French Empire spanned across the world and once controlled over half of Africa
